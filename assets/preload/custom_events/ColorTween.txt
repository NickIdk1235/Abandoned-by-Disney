Value1 = Sprite/Character you want to color
Value2 = color,time,ease
if v2 is empty, the value made for it will be
white,0.5,linear
in v2, put the color first, then time, then ease
if ease is empty it'll just be linear ease
colors shortcuts available will be the colors are listed below:
red, yellow, green, cyan, blue, purple, pink,
black, grey, orange, white, gray, lightgray, darkgray, crimson, brown, brownorange
you can also just put in the hexcode for what color you want to use
